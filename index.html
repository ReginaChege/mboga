About mbogaGo

Mbogago is a website that provides a platform for local farmers, aka Mama Mboga, to showcase their high-quality

produce to potential customers in East Africa. Mbogago emphasizes the importance of building relationships with

customers and providing personalized recommendations. They are committed to sustainability and eco-friendliness

by using locally sourced produce to reduce their carbon footprint and support local farmers. Mbogago aims to honor

the tradition of Mama Mboga as a staple in East African markets and is dedicated to providing a personal touch to

their business.
